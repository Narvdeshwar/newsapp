Description du produit

An ancient Indian hair secret, this cult-favourite HoliRoots Pre-Wash Strengthening Treatment Hair Oil includes strengthening ashwagandha for a healthier-looking and beautiful mane.



The HoliRoots Hair Oil is an active blend of holistic plant roots, such as Indian adaptogen ashwagandha, to strengthen the scalp and roots. The lightweight texture is formulated for all hair types including coloured hair.

Key ingredients:
- Dashmool: The 'ten roots' that are the ultimate remedy for calming the scalp.
- Ashwagandha: Indian adaptogen that strengthens & supports natural scalp circulation.
- Castor Oil: Thickens hair, nourishes and restores damaged hair.

Olfacotry notes: A spicy floral oriental scent blending precious red champaca and floral jasmine sambac. Be transported to the divine city of Madurai in India.



Vegan, Cruelty-free and formulated without silicones.

Conseils d'utilisation

- Part dry hair.

- Fill dropper with desired amount of oil and rub between palms.

- Apply oil to scalp and hair. Massage into roots, focusing on the crown.

- Leave on for at least five minutes or overnight for best results.

- Rinse well with lukewarm water.
